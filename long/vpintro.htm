FIEV genesis:

PFIEV was created in 1997 under the aegis of President Jacques Chirac and officialized by draft agreement. It was inaugurated November 12th, 1999, under the patronage of Vice President Nguyen Thi Binh and H.E.Serge Degallaix, French Ambassador.
